Definition of artificial intelligence

artificial intelligence is a system's ability to correctly interpret external data, to learn from such data, and to use those learning to achieve specific goals and tasks through flexible adaptation.



Application of artificial intelligent

  • Healthcare: Artificial intelligence is assisting doctors
  • Automotive: the creation and evolution of self-driving vehicles
  • Finance and economics Financial institutions have long used artificial neural network systems to detect charges or claims outside of the norm
